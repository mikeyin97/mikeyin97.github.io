A Bit About Me

Welcome to my website! My name is Michael Yin and I'm a second year engineering science student at the University of Toronto. I have an interest in computer programming, machine learning, and robotics, which are career paths I'm looking into for my future. I love participating in design competitions, doing Arduino projects, and implementing web apps. In my (little) free time, I enjoy playing sports, playing with my cats, coding, playing video games, watching anime, and reading.

The motto I live by is to strive to be better than the person I was yesterday. Each day, I push myself in my academic and professional endeavours as to improve myself, in terms of attitude, determination, and perseverance.
